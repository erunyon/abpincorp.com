Phlebotomy Skills Training:
CLSI Venipuncture and Skin Puncture Procedures


 


Syllabus

Phlebotomy Review

Target Audience: A workshop for any healthcare professional who wants a review of the latest phlebotomy guidelines or to prepare for a national certification examination (Phlebotomist, Phlebotomy Instructor/Supervisor, Laboratory Personnel, Medical Ass,., Nurse, Patient Care Technician).

Objectives

  • CLSI guidelines for performing venipunctures and skin puncture (finger stick and heel puncture)
  • Complications associated with phlebotomy performance and patient conditions
  • Tube collection order of draw and order of collect
  • Legal issues and terminology relating to phlebotomy
  • Latest OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens Standard
  • Causes of burnout, interventions and ways to prevent it
  • Venipuncture technique during hands-on instruction
  • Age specific phlebotomy concerns
