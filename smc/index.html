Thank you for your interest in our ECG and Phlebotomy Technician training programs. Clinical laboratories, hospitals, doctor's offices, home health care agencies, extended care facilities and paramedical services all use skilled professionals. A successful completion of ABP's approved programs will allow you to apply for and take a national certification examination. Many employers are now requiring a phlebotomist to be certified for employment. With a certification, you can currently work in any state.

Our classes are taught in January, May and September. Only completed applications received will be called for interviews.

The ECG Technician class is held for 7 days, 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM daily. Since this is a focused class, attendance is required each day. If you successfully complete this course, you will receive 4 credit hours from Southwestern Michigan College. The cost of this course is $1000. We pay your tuition to SMC out of what you pay us. The course cost also includes the lab fees, lab supplies and textbook.

The Phlebotomy Technician course is focused, attendance is required each day. The class is held from 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM for three consecutive weeks. If you successfully complete the classroom and student laboratory portion of the training, you will then spend 120 hours at a local clinical laboratory gaining the experience and skills necessary. A national certification exam will be offered at the end of this class.

We are pleased to offer college credit through Southwestern Michigan College for this course. You will need to be eligible to be admitted as a student to SMC even though the classes will be taught at our facility in Granger, Indiana. The phlebotomy training course is 9 credits and costs $2,450. This cost includes your tuition, lab fees, lab supplies, liability insurance and textbook.

Please complete the training school application and send to ABP as soon as possible. After your application is received, we will telephone you for your personal interview. A maximum of 6 students are included in each class and they tend to fill fast.

Please call us at 574-277-0691 if we can answer any other questions for you. We look forward to hearing from you soon!
