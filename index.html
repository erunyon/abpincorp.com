An innovative leader in the field of healthcare training, ABP Inc. provides education and hands-on training for the healthcare provider, professional and educator. The faculty of ABP believes the solution to meeting the needs of today's healthcare providers lies in training, cross-training and education. As a means of achieving this goal, ABP offers these services: Phlebotomy Technician training and skill upgrades, ECG Technician training and skills upgrade, Patient Care Technician training. ABP teaches these courses at a local community college, their facility or yours. Several seminars are scheduled throughout the year to provide continuing education opportunities to healthcare professionals. Custom seminars or hands-on workshops are also developed to meet your specific needs.

ABP, Inc. is an approved provider of continuing education programs in the clinical laboratory sciences by the ASCLS P.A.C.E.® Program.
ABP is committed to meeting the changing and dynamic needs of the healthcare professional throughout the development of industry partnerships. Review manuals as well as review sessions are available to prepare for certification examinations in phlebotomy,  ECG,  and patient care technician.
