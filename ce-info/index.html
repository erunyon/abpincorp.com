So, you need some continuing education hours for recertification or because your employer requires it. We have a solution for you - and you can do it at your convenience! We offer CE packets that you may order as you need them.

The ASCLS (American Society for Clinical Laboratory Scientists) has approved ABP, LLC. as a provider of continuing education programs in the clinical laboratory sciences through the P.A.C.E.® program (#409). ABP, LLC is also approved as a continuing education provider for California clinical laboratory licensees under the P.A.C.E.® (#OOO1) California accrediting agency license. P.A.C.E.® is recognized by all national certification agencies for recertification requirements as well as meeting state license renewal requirements.

Continuing Education Order Form is available that you may use to order topics that are of interest to you. Currently, we have packets on phlebotomy, ECG technique and interpretations, medical coding, OSHA topics, ergonomics and management. You may select any number of packets and send in payment for those at any time.

Upon receipt of the completed form and payment, the articles and quizzes you have selected will be mailed to you. Participants need to read the article, take the quiz, circle the answers on the CE Registration Form and then send it back to us. If at least 7O% of the answers are correct, a certificate of continuing education credit will be mailed to you.

Please call if you have any more questions. ABP looks forward to assisting you maintain your skills by continuing education!
